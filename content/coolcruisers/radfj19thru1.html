  • All Radiators have 4 BIG Rows

    Don't buy the cheaper 3 Row radiator offered by others and aluminum radiators cannot be repaired.

    CCOT's new 4 Row aftermarket Radiators meet or beat original equipment specifications and performance. Our radiators match or exceed the cooling capacity of your Land Cruiser's original radiator and with a guaranteed fit.

    CCOT has sold thousands of these radiators with excellent Customer satisfaction. CCOT sells about 25 of these aftermarket radiators per month and has done so over the past 10 years.

    CCOT aftermarket Radiators are direct replacements for Toyota radiators.
