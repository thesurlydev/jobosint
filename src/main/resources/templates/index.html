Personal job management and tools app.

Feature Roadmap

  • Scheduled job search (LinkedIn)
  • Augment the company detail view with: jobs, applications and contacts
  • Company Reviews from: Indeed, TeamBlind, Glassdoor, etc.
  • Determine the careers page for a company
  • Cover Letter generator
  • Companies previously applied to
  • Layoff information
  • Company news
  • Interview process
  • Resume tweaks/versions based on base resume and job description
  • Notifications
  • Dashboard
    • Metrics
      • Number of jobs applied for this week
      • Total number of: applications, jobs, companies, contacts
    • Recent Activity
      • Newly discovered jobs
      • Recently filled jobs
